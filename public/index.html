About us

One partner. Complete delivery.

ALAI is a Norwegian digital agency that builds, deploys, and maintains software from A to Z. From the first pixel to production server — one partner, complete delivery.

We use the same AI we build for clients. That means faster delivery, lower cost, and fewer errors. Humans set the direction. AI does the rest.
